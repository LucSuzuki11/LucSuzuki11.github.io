Automated Parking Lot System

November 2024

This system models the real life workings of tolls booths in parking lots and garages. Equipt with two lanes - in and out - this system can process vehicles in both directions. Using servo motors with a security lever in combination with an ultra-sonic sensor, vehicles can be detected and let through. This system comes with two forms of payment one being a manual payment (button) and another is card payemnt (RFID).
